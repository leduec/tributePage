Emanuel Lasker
Picture of Emanuel Lasker.

Emanuel Lasker was born in 1868 in Germany and died 1941 in New York.

He was a german chess player, mathematician and philosoph. He became the first and at the same time only german chess world champion so far. He kept this title for 27 years (1894 till 1921). No other chess player was as long WCC as he was. In the year 2008 Emanuel Lasker was inducted into the Hall of Fame of German Sports.
